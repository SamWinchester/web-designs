About Kalanjali

Sir M.Visvesvaraya Institute Of Technology, Bengaluru has been conducting
Kalanjali an annual inter-collegiate cultural extravaganza successfully since the year 1996.
This year, in addition to the cultural events, Kalanjali includes technical competitions as well.

Kalanjali has been setting the stage for the student community to show case their talent,
innovation and creativity with zeal and zest. Kalanjali 2014 is scheduled to be held on the
05th October and 06th October 2014.
